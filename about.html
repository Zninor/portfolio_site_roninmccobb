About

my story

Hello, my name is Ronin McCobb and I’m a UX/UI Designer based in San Francisco. I’ve lived in the Bay Area for most of my life, and attended UC Berkeley to get my Bachelor of Arts in Global Studies. There, I studied interactions between states, societies, and cultures around the globe in order to understand how complex international problems come to be and how they can be solved. I also spent a lot of time working on art in my freetime, from sculptures and printmaking to graphic design for school clubs and events.

After graduating, I decided that a career in UX/UI design best paired by creative design skills with my desire to solve problems with the stakeholder or user’s perspective in mind. This led me to completing a UI Design course with CareerFoundry after I graduated, where I learned design principles, wireframing, prototyping, and how to design elegant solutions for everyday user problems.
